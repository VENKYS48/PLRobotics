SR-1



PLR developed the SR-1 model which is a light weight SCARA model having 4 DOF(degree of freedom), a wider range of 245mm and vertical reach of 100mm.
It covers an angle of 270 degrees which makes it best suitable for electronics assembly, pick and place and many more.
