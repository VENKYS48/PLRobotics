SCARA

SCARA (Selective Compliance Assembly Robot Arm) robots represent a crucial innovation in industrial automation. SCARA robots have a unique structure optimized for specific tasks, particularly those requiring precise horizontal movements.
Design and Functionality: SCARA robots have a vertical arm that moves horizontally in a plane, while the end-effector can move vertically. This configuration allows for fast and precise movement within a confined workspace, making them ideal for tasks such as assembly, pick-and-place operations, packaging, and more.



Articulated Robotic Arm

Not only that, articulated robots have three more joints than the human arm, giving them the ability to reach just about any area within their work envelope.
